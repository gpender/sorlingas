Electronic Charting Solutions

Sorlingas Data Services supply bespoke electronic navigational products to the maritime and port sector.

The company specialises in producing Bathymetric ENCs (bENCs) and Berthing Scale (Band 6) ENCs, but all S-57 product commissions are considered including larger scale charts.

James Wright founded the company in 2019 having had previous experience working at the UK Hydrographic Office, then helping implement a full electronic chart portfolio at the Port of London.
